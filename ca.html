CPU & Architecture

The Central Processing Unit (CPU) is the brain. Key components include:

  • ALU: Arithmetic Logic Unit (Calculations).
  • CU: Control Unit (Coordinates activities).
  • Registers: PC (Program Counter), MAR (Memory Address), MDR (Memory Data), CIR (Current Instruction), and Accumulator.

Memory & Storage

RAM: Volatile, fast, main memory.
ROM: Non-volatile, stores BIOS.
